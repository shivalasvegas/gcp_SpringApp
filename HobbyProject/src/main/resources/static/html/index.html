About Us...She Felt Good is Shiva King.

A throwaway comment by an art teacher seven years ago led me on a scientific and creative journey into dyeing and creating felted objects. My first interest was in creating wet felted pictures using water colour techniques. I soon found out that I need to learn needle felting as well. So here I am still experimenting with natural dyes and felting with different types of fleeces. My website is a creative window on my world and where my journey takes me. I live in Torrington with my faithful and often bizarre doggy companion Pip. Some of my other interests include fossil hunting, wild swimming, and coding... in fact now all my life is mostly coding, with the occasional sleep and time to eat....
